# Retool — State of Internal Tools 2023

- **Source URL**: https://retool.com/blog/state-of-internal-tools-2023
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20
- **Sample**: 2,276 respondents (>50% developers and engineering leaders), March 2023

## Time on Internal Tools

- **86%** of respondents spent as much or more time on internal tools vs. prior year
- Upward trajectory now spans ALL company sizes (solos to 5,000+ employees)
- **86%** expect internal tools investment to increase or hold steady over next 12 months
- Companies in **Consulting**, Financial Services, Data/Analytics, and Energy sectors: **89-90%** increased spending

## Historical Context (from 2021 report, 650 respondents)

- Developers spend **more than 30%** of their time building internal applications
- At companies with **5,000+ employees**: jumps to **45%** of dev time on internal tools

## Productivity Impact

- Platform users were **20.5% more likely** to feel a productivity boost
- **22%** blamed poor productivity on excessive context switching between tools
- At **enterprise companies (5,000+)**: context switching complaints rose to **33%**
- Among **security professionals**: **37%** cited context switching as a productivity drain
