## Time Metrics
- 44% of organizations report onboarding takes over two months
- 43% of developers using AI say onboarding takes less than a month (vs. 20% without AI)

## Key Quotes on Onboarding Challenges
- "Nearly half (44%) of organizations say onboarding new developers takes more than two months"
- Setup includes "Installing and configuring development environments" and obtaining "user access to server-side tools, like version control, issue tracking, CI/CD, and security tools"

## Hidden Costs
- Low employee engagement costs the global economy $8.9 trillion annually (9% of global GDP) per Gallup's 2024 State of the Global Workplace report
- Poor onboarding correlates with "poor performance, disengagement, and low retention"

## Productivity Impact
The article emphasizes that inefficient onboarding leads to "reduced productivity, higher turnover rates, and potential security risks" but doesn't cite specific productivity loss percentages.

## AI's Effect
Survey data shows substantial differences: developers with AI access report significantly faster onboarding (less than one month) compared to those without AI assistance (20%).

## Bottleneck Areas
Common delays stem from fragmented toolchains, unclear security protocols, and difficulty navigating multiple systems for access and permissions.
