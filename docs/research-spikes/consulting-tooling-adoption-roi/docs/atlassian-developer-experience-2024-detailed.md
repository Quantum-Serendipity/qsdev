# Atlassian — State of Developer Experience 2024 (Detailed)

- **Source URL**: https://www.getint.io/blog/state-of-developer-experience-report-2024-summary
- **Original report**: https://www.atlassian.com/software/compass/resources/state-of-developer-2024
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20
- **Methodology**: 1,250 engineering leaders surveyed by Wakefield Research; 900 developers surveyed by DX across multiple countries

## Developer Productivity Loss

- **69%** of developers report losing **8+ hours per week** to inefficiencies (= 20% of capacity)
- **97%** of developers lose significant time to inefficiencies overall

## Main Friction Points

- Technical debt
- Insufficient documentation
- Complex build processes
- Lack of focus time
- Unclear direction

## Leadership vs Developer Perception Gap

- **Less than 50%** of developers believe leadership understands these inefficiencies
- **99%** of leaders acknowledge increasing complexity in the developer role
- Leaders view hours worked, code quantity, story points as ineffective metrics

## Developer Satisfaction & Retention

- **63%** consider developer experience essential for job retention decisions
- **Only 23%** satisfied with organization's investment in improving DX
- **86%** of leaders recognize DX importance for talent attraction/retention

## AI Impact

- **Only 38%** report moderate-to-significant productivity gains from AI tools
- Developers more skeptical than leaders about AI benefits
