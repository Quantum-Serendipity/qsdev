# Cortex: The 2024 State of Developer Productivity
- **Source URL**: https://www.cortex.io/report/the-2024-state-of-developer-productivity
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20
- **Note**: Content extracted via WebFetch; may be summarized from original

## Survey Methodology
- **Sample Size:** 50 engineering leaders
- **Company Size:** 500+ employees
- **Geographic Coverage:** North America, Europe, AsiaPac
- **Question Types:** Free-text and multiple choice

## Key Onboarding Metrics

**Time to First Meaningful Contributions:**
- 72% of respondents indicated "more than 1 month" for new hires to submit their first 3 meaningful PRs
- 54% cited 1-3 months as the typical duration
- 18% reported over 3 months

## Productivity Loss Data

**Weekly Hours Lost to Unproductive Work:**
- 58% report more than 5 hours per developer per week lost
- 54% estimate 5-15 hours per developer per week
- Only 10% believe losses are under 3 hours
- Those losing 15+ hours per week do not use Internal Developer Portals

## Root Causes of Onboarding Delays

**Primary Blockers:**
- "Gathering project context" ranked as #1 productivity leak (26% tied with approval delays)
- 40% of developers cite difficulty finding critical context
- Those reporting 3+ months onboarding consistently identified context-gathering as their top blocker

**IDP Usage Impact:**
- Non-IDP users: 48% struggle with context discovery
- Backstage IDP users: 86% still report context-finding challenges
- Zero IDP users reported data consistency issues vs. 21% of non-IDP organizations
