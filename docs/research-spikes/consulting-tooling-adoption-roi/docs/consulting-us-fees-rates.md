# Consulting Fees & Rates (US Market)
- **Source**: https://www.consulting.us/consulting-industry/fees-rates
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Revenue Per Consultant (Annual Proxy for Rates)

**Global Strategy Consulting Firms (MBB & peers)**
- Highest rates in consulting; specific figures not disclosed
- McKinsey (~17,000 employees), BCG, Bain, Strategy&, Roland Berger, A.T. Kearney

**Global Functional Specialists**
- Range: $300,000-$400,000 per year
- Examples: Hay Group, Mercer, Willis Towers Watson, FTI Consulting, Navigant

**Big Four (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG)**
- Same range as Functional Specialists ($300K-$400K annually)
- Strategy/management practices command premium; lower for audit/IT ops

**National/Regional Functional Specialists**
- Similar to Big Four, dependent on reputation and focus

**Mid-Market Players**
- Range: $150,000-$220,000 per year
- Includes: Small/mid-size tech firms, mid-size global accountants (BDO, Baker Tilly, Grant Thornton, RSM, PKF), global technology firms (Atos, Accenture ~370,000 employees, Capgemini, CGI)

**Independent Contractors**
- Range: Below $50,000 to above $400,000 annually
