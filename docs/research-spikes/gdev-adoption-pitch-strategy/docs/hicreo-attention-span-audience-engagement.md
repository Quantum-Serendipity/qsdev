- **Retrieved**: 2026-05-15

## Key Definition
Attention span is the amount of time someone can focus on a task without becoming distracted, a challenge in today's digital environment.

## Primary Factors Affecting Attention

1. **Digital distractions** — addressed by starting with impactful openings
2. **Information overload** — managed through strategic use of visuals
3. **Monotone delivery** — countered with storytelling and energetic presentation

## Strategies to Maintain Attention

- Strong introductions with compelling stories or questions
- Visual storytelling using images, videos, and slides
- Interactive elements including polls, Q&A sessions, and audience activities
- Varied vocal delivery with strategic pausing
- Purposeful body language and eye contact
