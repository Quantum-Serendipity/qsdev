- **Note**: Industry generalizations, no specific survey citations or sample sizes provided

## Productivity Timeline Claims

- Standard onboarding: 4-6 weeks until new developers become productive
- Structured onboarding: 2-3 weeks to meaningful productivity; 30-45 days for full independence
- Fast teams: Environment setup in 15 minutes
- Slow teams: Environment setup in 2-3 days

## Time to First Contribution

- Fast teams: day 3-5 for first meaningful code merge
- Slow teams: week 2-3 for first commit

## Environment Setup Waste

- Developers spend 2-5 days troubleshooting configuration issues that were already solved

## Retention Impact

- Organizations with poor onboarding lose 25% of technical hires within first year
- 86% of new hires decide whether they'll stay long-term within 6 months
- Structured onboarding improves retention from 60-70% to 85%+ at year one
- 40% reduction in first-year attrition
- Replacing a lost developer costs $200-300K
