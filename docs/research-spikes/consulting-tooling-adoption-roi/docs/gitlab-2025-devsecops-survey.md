# GitLab 2025 Global DevSecOps Survey — "The AI Paradox"

- **Source URL**: https://www.stocktitan.net/news/GTLB/git-lab-survey-reveals-the-ai-paradox-faster-coding-creates-new-dkxj7t66f5f5.html
- **Press release**: https://about.gitlab.com/press/releases/2025-11-10-gitlab-survey-reveals-the-ai-paradox/
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20
- **Conducted by**: The Harris Poll for GitLab
- **Sample**: 3,266 DevSecOps professionals (IT ops, security, software dev)

## Productivity Loss

- **7 hours per week** lost to inefficient processes per team member
- Root causes: lack of cross-functional communication, limited knowledge sharing, fragmented tool ecosystems

## Tool Fragmentation

- **60%** use more than 5 tools for software development
- **49%** use more than 5 AI tools simultaneously
- **82%** deploy to production at least weekly

## Platform Engineering

- **85%** agree agentic AI succeeds best via platform engineering approaches
- **97%** are using or planning to use AI in software development lifecycle

## Code Quality

- **73%** experienced problems from "vibe coding" (prompting without understanding)
- **88%** agree human qualities like creativity remain irreplaceable

## Compliance

- **70%** report AI complicates compliance management
- **76%** discover compliance issues post-deployment rather than during development
