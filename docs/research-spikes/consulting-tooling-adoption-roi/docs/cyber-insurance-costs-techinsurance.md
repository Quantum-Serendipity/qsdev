# Cybersecurity Insurance Costs for Consulting Firms & Tech Companies

## Average Monthly Premiums

Based on TechInsurance data:

- **Tech Errors & Omissions (E&O)**: $83 per month or $990 yearly
- **Cyber Liability Insurance**: $150 per month or $1,799 annually
- **General Liability**: $30 per month or $365 yearly
- **Fidelity Bonds**: $89 per month or $1,067 annually
- **Workers' Compensation**: $34 per month or $411 annually
- **Commercial Auto**: $198 per month or $2,375 yearly

## Typical Coverage Details

**Tech E&O Policy Example:**
- Premium: $83/month
- Limits: $1 million per occurrence; $1 million aggregate
- Deductible: $2,500

**General Liability Policy Example:**
- Premium: $30/month
- Limits: $1 million per occurrence; $2 million aggregate
- Deductible: $500

## Business Owner's Policy Bundle
Cybersecurity companies averaging a business owner's policy at $46 per month ($550 yearly) when bundling general liability with commercial property coverage.

## What's Covered

These policies protect against professional negligence claims, data breaches, third-party cyber incidents, bodily injury, property damage, and employee dishonesty through fidelity bonds.
