## Financial Costs
- **Average onboarding cost:** $1,830 per employee (Leena AI)
- **SMB range:** $600-$1,800 per employee
- **Large organizations:** Over $3,000 per employee
- **Hiring cost:** ~$4,425 per new employee (SHRM, BambooHR)
- **Replacement cost:** 21% of annual salary (Center for American Progress)
- **Soft costs ratio:** ~60% of total hiring/onboarding expenses; hard costs ~40% (BambooHR)

## Time-Based Metrics
- **Automation savings:** 5 days reduced onboarding time (Digitate)
- **eSignature efficiency:** 40 hours saved monthly for 100-employee company (BambooHR)
- **Average vacancy fill time:** 36 days
- **Early turnover:** 20% quit within first 45 days (Harvard Business Review)
- **Texas Instruments case:** New employees reached full productivity 2 months faster with updated onboarding

## Productivity Impact
- **Productivity increase:** 60% improvement with effective onboarding (SHRM)
- **Revenue growth:** 2.5x increase (Karolinska Institute)
- **Profit growth:** 1.5x increase (Karolinska Institute)
- **Retention boost:** 52% improvement (SHRM)
- **Salesforce loss:** $35 million in productivity from 5-day onboarding
- **Amazon loss:** $678 million over 2 years

## Engagement & Retention
- **Dedication increase:** 18x more committed employees (BambooHR)
- **3+ year retention:** 69% with exceptional onboarding (SHRM)
- **Poor onboarding departures:** 9% (HRD America, Paychex)
