## Duration and Structure
The onboarding program lasts **one week** and includes all new developers (interns and full-time employees) starting on the same day.

## Key Phases

**Pre-Onboarding:**
New hires receive access to executive recordings covering "product direction, engineering infrastructure, internal processes" and are introduced to development tools before their first day.

**Onboarding Week Activities:**
- Tech talks on code style, infrastructure, and company values
- Hands-on workshops called "Code Labs" that provide step-by-step walkthroughs
- Real bug fixes shipped to production during the week
- Meetings with engineering leadership

## Productivity Metrics

The article notes improved outcomes: "Instead of spending the first month ramping [our intern] up on our tools and best practices, I could instead spend the time ramping him up on our application-specific problem sets," per Sean French, a Platform Dev lead.

Jerome Cornet, Director of Engineering, emphasizes that foundational preparation enables faster specialization: "by the end the team can focus on teaching new employees about the problem they are solving rather than the mechanics of shipping effectively."

## Notable Outcomes
Developers transition directly to their assigned teams after the week and immediately contribute to ongoing features and products.
