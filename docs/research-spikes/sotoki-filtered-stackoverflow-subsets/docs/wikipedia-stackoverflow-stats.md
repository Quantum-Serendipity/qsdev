## Key Statistics

- As of June 2025: over 29 million registered users, over 24 million questions and 36 million answers
- Top eight most-discussed topics by tag: JavaScript, Java, C#, PHP, Android, Python, jQuery, HTML
- Questions submitted fell 78% between December 2024 and December 2025
- Peak was 200,000 questions per month in 2014
