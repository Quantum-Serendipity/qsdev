# Billable Rates 101: A Guide For Professional Services
- **Source**: https://www.scoro.com/blog/billable-rate/
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Key Formulas

**Average Cost Per Hour (ACPH):**
ACPH = Total Annual Costs / Total Annual Capacity

**Hourly Billable Rate:**
ACPH / (1 - Delivery Margin Target) = Hourly Billable Rate

**Average Billable Rate (ABR):**
Total Billable Revenue / Total Hours Worked = ABR

## Concrete Example

Five-person agency:
- Total annual costs: $397,000
- Annual billable capacity hours: 8,575
- ACPH: $46/hour
- With 70% delivery margin target: $46 / (1 - 0.70) = $153/hour billable rate
- Implicit multiplier: approximately 3.3x ($153 / $46)

## Target Delivery Margin

Recommended: 70% delivery margin to cover costs, generate sustainable profit, and absorb workload fluctuations.

## Overhead Components

Annual salaries, benefits (health insurance, retirement contributions, etc.), overhead (rent, utilities, software subscriptions, etc.), and any regular freelance or consultant costs should be included when calculating total annual costs.
