# Data Breach Cost Statistics for Small Businesses

## Overall Average Costs
Small businesses can expect to pay $120,000 to $1.24M in 2025 to respond and resolve an incident.

## Cost Components

**Ransomware Demands:**
The typical ransom request has escalated significantly. The average cost of ransomware demand is $1.54M, almost double the $812,380 average in 2022.

**Employee Hours & Meetings:**
- Executive strategy sessions: $500-$1,000 per hour per participant
- Single executive meeting (6 people, 1 hour): $3,000-$5,000
- IT/Security team meetings: $75-$150 per hour per person
- 8-person, 2-hour technical meeting: $1,200-$2,400

**Professional Services:**
Incident response retainers vary substantially: $800 to $1,000 per hour for some, while others might charge between $300 to $500 per hour, with total breach response costs potentially exceeding $100,000.

**Communications & PR:**
Crisis communication meetings typically involve participants at $150-$300 hourly rates, with 2-hour meetings costing $1,800-$3,600.

## Compliance Penalties

**HIPAA:** Up to $2,067,813 annually across violation tiers; Tier 4 maximum reaches $1,500,000.

**PCI DSS:** $5,000-$10,000 monthly initially; escalating to $50,000-$100,000 monthly after six months.

**GDPR:** EUR 10 million or 2% of annual revenue (minimum); EUR 20 million or 4% annual turnover (maximum).

**CCPA:** $2,500 per violation; $7,500 for intentional violations.
