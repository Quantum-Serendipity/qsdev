# Billable Utilization Rate Statistics in Professional Services Firms
- **Source**: https://www.mosaicapp.com/post/billable-utilization-rate-statistics-in-professional-services-firms
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Global Benchmarks

- **Overall industry average (2023):** 68.9% utilization across worldwide professional services organizations
- **Historically optimal range:** 70-75%
- **Current "Goldilocks Zone":** 70-80% (per SPI 2025 PS Maturity Benchmark)

## Industry-Specific Targets

**Higher utilization sectors:**
- Management consulting, IT services, engineering: exceeding 80%

**Moderate utilization sectors:**
- Accounting, advertising, architecture, research: ~70%

**Lower utilization sectors:**
- Healthcare, construction, education: 40-70%

## Role-Based Targets

- Producers/Freelancers: 75-80%
- Managers/Senior roles: 35-50%
- Optimal non-billable time: 20-25% for client-facing roles

## Historical Trend

Declining trend from traditional 75% benchmarks toward lower averages (68.9%), reflecting industry recognition that excessive utilization (exceeding 80%) causes employee burnout and attrition.
