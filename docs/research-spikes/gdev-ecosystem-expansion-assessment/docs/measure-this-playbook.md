## Summary

A playbook for running pre/post measurement around team learning experiments using the Learning Opportunities skill. Provides validated survey instruments from peer-reviewed research (n=3,267 and n=1,282 professional developers).

## Core Survey Items (6 items, ~2 minutes)

1. Learning Culture (DTS-LC) — 2 items, 5-point Likert
2. AI Skill Threat (PAST) — 2 items, 5-point Likert
3. Coding Self-Efficacy (CSE) — 1 item, 5-point Likert
4. AI Behavioral Action (AI-BA) — 1 item, 5-point scale

## Optional Add-Ons (longer timeframe needed)
5. Sense of Belonging (M-SBS) — 2 items
6. Developer Agency (DTS-A) — 2 items
7. Team Effectiveness (TER) — 1 item

## Key Guidance
- Pre/post design, 1-week minimum
- Keep anonymous when possible
- Use exact same items/scales both times
- Look at direction, not magnitude for small teams
- Do NOT fixate on p-values for teams of 5-8
- Do NOT treat individual scores as diagnostic
- Use results as discussion prompts
- Compare to published study averages as talking points
