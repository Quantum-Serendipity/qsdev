## Daily Cost Calculation Formula

> "(Annual Salary + Overhead Costs) / Billable Days Per Year"

## Specific Example

- Annual Salary: $130,000
- Overhead Costs: $40,000
- Billable Days Per Year: 220
- **Daily Cost: $773**

The consultant generates revenue at $2,000 daily, making the true opportunity cost $2,773 per day.

## Utilization Rate Benchmarks

According to the 2024 Professional Services Maturity Benchmark by SPI Research:
- Typical consulting firm average: **73%**
- Highest performing firms: **80%**
- Optimal non-billable allocation: **20%**

## Revenue Impact Analysis

The gap between 73% and 80% utilization over 220 billable days equals 15 days annually, translating to:
- **$30,000 additional annual revenue** per consultant
- **Over $40,000 total value** to the firm per consultant
- For a 20-person team: **$600,000 in additional annual revenue**
