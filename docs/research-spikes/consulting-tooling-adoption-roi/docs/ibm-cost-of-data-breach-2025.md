# Key Statistics from IBM 2025 Cost of Data Breach Report

## Average Breach Costs
- **Global average:** $4.44 million (first decline in five years)
- **U.S. average:** $10.22 million per incident (all-time high)

## Costs by Industry
- **Healthcare:** $7.42 million (highest average)
- **Financial Services:** $5.56 million (second highest)

## Costs by Attack Vector
- **Insider threats:** $4.92 million (highest among initial threat vectors)
- **Phishing:** Most common initial attack vector at 16% of attacks
- **Supply chain compromise** and **compromised credentials:** Rounded out top three vectors

## AI-Related Breaches
- "One in six organizations experienced breaches involving AI-driven attacks"
- Phishing via AI: 37%
- Deepfake impersonations: 35%
- Shadow AI incidents: 20% of breaches (7% higher than sanctioned AI incidents)
- Organizations lacking proper AI access controls: 97%
- Organizations without AI governance policies: 63%

## Recovery Timeline
- 76% required over 100 days for full recovery
- Only 2% recovered in under 50 days

## Regulatory Impact
- 32% of surveyed breaches resulted in regulatory fines
- 48% of fines exceeded $100,000

## Ransomware Response
- 63% refused payment (up from 59% in 2024)
- Only 40% contacted law enforcement (down from 52%)
