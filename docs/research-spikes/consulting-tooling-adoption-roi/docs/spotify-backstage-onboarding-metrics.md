## Onboarding Time Improvement

The most significant metric: **time-to-10th-PR (pull request)**:

- **Before Backstage**: 60 days
- **After Backstage**: Less than 20 days

This represents a reduction of approximately 67% in the time required for new engineers to make meaningful contributions.

## Additional Context

Pia Nilsson, Platform Developer Experience Tribe lead, noted that before Backstage implementation, "our onboarding metrics kept ballooning upward which meant new hires weren't able to meaningfully contribute for quite a while," despite rapid hiring during Spotify's growth phase around 2016.
