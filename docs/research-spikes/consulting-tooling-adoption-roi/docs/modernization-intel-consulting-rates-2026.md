## Independent Contractor Benchmark

A 2025 global survey of 3,571 independent IT professionals found consulting/management specialists averaged EUR 120/hour (~$130/hr). Independent practitioners with 8+ years are advised to establish minimum rates at $150-200/hour.

## Market Trends

Growing demand for AI integration, cloud-native re-architecture, and mainframe offload has pushed specialist rates 8-15% above 2024 levels.
