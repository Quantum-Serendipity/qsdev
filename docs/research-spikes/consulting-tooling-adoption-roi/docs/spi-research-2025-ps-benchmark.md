# SPI Research 2025 Professional Services Maturity Benchmark
- **Source**: https://spiresearch.com/reports/2025-ps-maturity-benchmark/ (report summary); search results from multiple SPI/Deltek sources
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Key 2024 Benchmark Data (from 403 firms, 150,000+ consultants, ~$60B collective PS revenue)

### Utilization
- Billable utilization fell to 68.9% in 2024
- Below the 75% optimal threshold
- 75% has been the widely accepted industry standard for optimal balance
- Historical SPI data shows 70-80% maximized profitability
- Billable utilization peaked at 73.2% in 2021, declining steadily since

### Revenue Per Consultant
- Fell to $199K in 2024
- Industry average from Mosaic: $204,000; top performers up to $270,000

### Profitability
- EBITDA fell to 9.8% in 2024 from 15.4% in 2023
- Lowest in 5 years

### Overhead (from Deltek Clarity A&E Study)
- Architecture firms reported 162% overhead rate
- Overhead multiplier = Total Overhead Expenses / Direct Labor Costs

### Survey Coverage
Firms from: IT Consulting, Management Consulting, Software & SaaS, Accounting, Marketing & Advertising, Architecture & Engineering

### Context
Decline reflects economic volatility and regulatory shifts in 2024.
