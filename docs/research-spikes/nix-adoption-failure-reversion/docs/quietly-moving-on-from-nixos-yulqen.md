# Quietly Moving on from NixOS?

**Author:** MR Lemon (Yulqen.org)
**Date:** April 22, 2023
**Duration of NixOS Use:** Approximately 6 months

## Reasons for Leaving
- Insufficient technical depth to troubleshoot problems outside standard configurations
- Lack of traditional Filesystem Hierarchy Standard makes running simple scripts overly complex
- Overlays require significant mental effort to implement and understand
- Two of three laptops became unusable due to hardware failures (battery and network card problems on Thinkpads x201 and x220), reducing the primary benefit of multi-machine reproducibility

## Planned Switch
Debian

## What They Appreciated
The author praised NixOS's declarative system management approach, describing how "adding a line of config here and there, commiting the change in your git repository and running sudo nixos-rebuild switch --flake .# is all it takes." They valued replicating identical configurations across multiple machines and managing Firefox settings through flakes.

## Overall Sentiment
Resigned disappointment. The author acknowledges NixOS "solves a real problem" but concludes: "I'm not clever enough and life is too short."
