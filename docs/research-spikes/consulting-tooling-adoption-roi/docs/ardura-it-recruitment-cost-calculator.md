## Productivity Ramp Curve

- **Month 1:** 25-40% of target efficiency
- **Months 2-3:** 60-80% productivity
- **Months 3-6:** Reaches productivity comparable to the person they replaced

## Onboarding Cost Breakdown

Total onboarding costs for a senior IT specialist: **70,000-90,000 PLN** (~$17,500-$22,500 USD), comprising:

- **Reduced productivity loss (first 3 months):** ~52,500 PLN
  - If monthly cost is 35,000 PLN and specialist works at 50% efficiency, loss = 52,500 PLN
- **Mentor/supervisor time (15-20% allocation):** 15,000-21,000 PLN
- **Training, equipment, and licenses:** 5,000-15,000 PLN

## Key Impact Metric

During onboarding, one new team member reduces the productivity of others by 15-20% in the first four weeks, indicating broader team efficiency impacts beyond the new hire's individual productivity curve.
