# KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026 Speaker Guide
- **Source**: https://events.linuxfoundation.org/kubecon-cloudnativecon-europe/program/speaker-guide/
- **Retrieved**: 2026-05-15

## Session Formats & Durations
- Lightning talks: 5 minutes maximum
- Breakout sessions & maintainer tracks: 30 minutes
- Tutorials: 75 minutes
- All durations include Q&A time

## Presentation Requirements

**Technical Specifications:**
- Speakers must provide their own laptops and HDMI adapters
- Slides formatted in 16:9 aspect ratio
- Present in Light Mode only (Dark Mode can distort colors)
- PDF version required for upload to Sched.com

**Content Guidelines:**
- Use official conference name: "KubeCon + CloudNativeCon Europe 2026"
- Download logos from official GitHub repositories
- Cannot use KubeCon or CloudNativeCon logos separately
- Comply with CNCF trademark policies
- Strict no-pitch, non-advertorial policy for all presentations, especially keynotes

## Technical Setup Tips

**Display Configuration:**
- Use "Extended Display" mode (not mirrored) for speaker notes visibility
- Close unnecessary apps and disable notifications beforehand
- Turn off Night Mode/TrueTone before connecting

**Recommended Practices:**
- Pre-record demos as backup for connectivity issues
- Provide download links in session descriptions
- Avoid activities requiring simultaneous online participation

## Special Session Types

**Lightning Talks:** Presented plug-and-play during the event with emcee introductions.

**Poster Sessions:** Digital format, JPEG, 3840x2160 resolution (minimum 1920x1080), under 10MB, landscape orientation, standard fonts at size 16+.

## Inclusivity & Code of Conduct
- Complete the "Inclusive Speaker Orientation" online course (collaboration with NCWIT)
- Review Code of Conduct carefully
- Ensure inclusive language and imagery in presentations

## Key Dates
- Speaker Registration: December 30
- Travel Funding Applications: January 25
- Presentation Upload Deadline: March 18
- Event Dates: March 24-26, 2026 at RAI Amsterdam
