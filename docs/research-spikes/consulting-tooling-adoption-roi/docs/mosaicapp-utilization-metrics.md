# The Utilization Metrics Every Consulting Firm Should Track
- **Source**: https://www.mosaicapp.com/post/the-utilization-metrics-every-consulting-firm-should-track
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Key Utilization Metrics

**Target Range:** 60-65% utilization rate is the healthy benchmark for consulting firms (conservative estimate).

**Definition:** Utilization rate measures the percentage of available employee hours spent on billable work.

## Financial Impact

Higher rates can boost revenue but risk employee burnout — direct correlation between utilization and profitability with operational tradeoffs.

Maintaining the 60-65% range ensures staff are neither underutilized nor overworked, balancing workload with revenue generation.

## Related Metrics

- **Average Revenue Per Consultant:** Industry average of $204,000, with top performers earning up to $270,000

## Strategic Implications

Optimization of utilization — without exceeding safe thresholds — represents a key lever for improving project profitability and overall firm financial performance.
