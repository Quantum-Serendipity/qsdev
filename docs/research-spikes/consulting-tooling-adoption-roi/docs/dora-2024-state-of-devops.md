# DORA 2024 State of DevOps Report — Key Findings

- **Source URL**: https://getdx.com/blog/2024-dora-report/
- **Original report**: https://dora.dev/research/2024/dora-report/
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Performance Cluster Distribution

- Elite cluster: Remained stable year-over-year
- High performance cluster: **Declined from 31% (2023) to 22% (2024)**
- Low performance cluster: **Grew from 17% to 25%**

## AI Impact

- **39.2%** of respondents expressed distrust in AI-generated code
- AI correlates with **worsened** software delivery performance (second consecutive year)
- **7.5% boost** potential in documentation quality if AI adoption increases by 25%
- Key finding: "AI tooling reduces time spent on meaningful work, but doesn't decrease time on toilsome tasks"

## Platform Engineering

- Internal platforms improve individual productivity and team performance
- Organizations using platforms typically deliver faster
- Platforms boost productivity while improving governance and security

## Quality vs Throughput

- Medium performance cluster showed lower change failure rates than high performers (first time)
- Both medium and high clusters: failure recovery times of **less than a day**

## DORA Core Metrics (General Benchmarks)

- Elite teams: deploy **multiple times per day**, lead time **less than one day**
- Low performers: deploy **monthly or less**, lead time **months**
