# Management Consulting Fees: How McKinsey Prices Projects
- **Source**: https://slideworks.io/resources/management-consulting-fees-how-mc-kinsey-prices-projects
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Billing Rates by Seniority Level (GSA Rate Cards)

### McKinsey (2024 GSA Rates)
- Senior Partner: $1,193.57/hour
- Engagement Manager: $834.40/hour
- Analyst/Associate: $327.41-$498.23/hour

### BCG (GSA Rates)
- Senior Partner: $1,116.17/hour
- Project Lead: $711.35/hour
- Senior Consultant: $629.35/hour
- Associate: $404.18/hour

### Kearney (GSA Rates)
- Project Leader: $624.00/hour
- Consultant: $421.00/hour

### Deloitte (GSA Rates)
- Consulting Management Executive: $373.00/hour
- Senior Consulting Staff: $257.62/hour

## Key Context

- Premium brands like McKinsey and BCG charge 2-3x the average consulting firm rate
- Average consulting firms charge in the $200-$300 range
- Top firms increasingly use fixed fees rather than hourly rates, structuring teams as "team bundles"
- GSA rate cards represent publicly disclosed pricing for government contracts and may not reflect standard commercial rates (commercial rates are often higher)
