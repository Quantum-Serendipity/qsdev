# Bench Time Costs: 5 Profit Leaks in IT Consulting
- **Source URL**: https://benchbee.io/blog/5-ways-your-it-consultancy-is-losing-money-on-bench-time/
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20
- **Note**: Content extracted via WebFetch; uses GBP figures (London-based consultancy examples)

## Direct Financial Impact

**Revenue Loss Per Consultant:**
- Average daily billing rate: £600-£1,000 per consultant
- Three senior consultants on bench: £48,000/month direct revenue loss
- Total monthly impact with overhead: £55,500

**Industry Benchmark:**
Top-performing consultancies maintain consultant utilisation rates of 80% or higher. Every percentage point below that benchmark directly impacts bottom line.

**Aggregate Firm Impact:**
A mid-sized consultancy with 50 consultants losing just 5% utilisation faces hundreds of thousands in lost annual revenue.

## Hidden Costs Beyond Lost Billable Hours

1. **Administrative overhead** (~£5,000/month for three benched consultants)
2. **Training and certification maintenance** (~£2,500/month)
3. **Opportunity costs** from delayed project starts
4. **Staff turnover**: Consultants experiencing >3 weeks bench time show 40% higher attrition rates
5. **Skill degradation**: "Technical skills have a half-life of just 18-24 months"

## Real-World Example

A London consultancy lost one month of billable time for two DevOps engineers:
- Lost revenue: £35,000
- Training costs: £3,000
- Replacement expense from departing engineer: Unspecified but material
- **Total: Over £50,000**

## Bench Time Standards

- **Maximum acceptable bench time:** 2 weeks per consultant
- **Target utilisation rate:** 80% minimum
- **Average bench period observed:** 3 weeks (poor management)
