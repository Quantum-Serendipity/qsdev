# IT Consulting Running Costs: $71K Monthly Fixed Overhead
- **Source**: https://financialmodelslab.com/blogs/operating-costs/it-consulting-services
- **Retrieved**: 2026-03-20

## Monthly Fixed Overhead

- Total Fixed Costs: $71,325/month (starting 2026)
- Payroll: $51,458/month for 50 full-time employees, including CEO at $180,000 annually
- Office/Utilities: $9,200/month (rent $8,000 + utilities/internet $1,200)
- Internal Tools: $2,200/month (CRM, project management software)
- Marketing Budget: $4,167/month ($50,000 annually)

## Financial Benchmarks

- Break-even Timeline: 18 months
- Year 1 EBITDA Loss: $437,000
- Minimum Cash Reserve Required: $287,000 through break-even
- Target Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) 2026: $2,500
- Required Lifetime Value (LTV) for 3:1 ratio: $7,500 minimum

## Note

This represents a startup IT consulting firm model. Established firms have different cost structures with higher margins.
